Public Transportation

(1) From New baiyun airport to Guangdong Hotel: about 30 kilometers, walk 115.0 meters to the airport south station subway station, take the subway line 3 north extension pass 3 stops, at jiahe wanggang station get off; Transfer from station to line 2, take subway line 2 to 10 stations and get off at the memorial station. About 462.0m walk to the terminal of Guangdong Hotel; Similar for the return trip.

新白云机场到广东大厦：约30公里，步行115.0米到机场南站地铁站， 乘坐地铁3号线北延段经过3站，在嘉禾望岗站下车； 站内换乘地铁2号线， 乘坐地铁2号线经过10站，在纪念堂站下车； 约步行462.0米便到达终点广东大厦；返程类似。

(2) Form Guangzhou railway station to Guangdong Hotel: about 2.5 kilometers, take subway line 2, pass 2 stops, reach the memorial station, walk about 600 meters, reach the Guangdong Hotel; Similar for the return trip.

广州火车站到广东大厦：约2.5公里，乘坐地铁2号线，经过2站, 到达纪念堂站，步行约600米，到达广东大厦；返程类似。
